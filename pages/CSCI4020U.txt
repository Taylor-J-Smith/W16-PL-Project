This course provides a detailed study of the compilation process for a procedural language. Students will develop an understanding of compiler design and put these principles into practice through the construction of a fully functioning compiler for a small 299procedural language using widely available tools for compiler construction and a general-purpose programming language. 3 cr, 3 lec, 1.5 lab. Prerequisite: CSCI 3020U. Credit restriction: SOFE3960U
